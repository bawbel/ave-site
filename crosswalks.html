The field has many scanners and frameworks and no shared vocabulary — independent studies find different tools barely agree on what they flag. AVE is the neutral reference they can all map to. These crosswalks let findings from SkillSpector, ClawScan, and OWASP AST10 all resolve to a common AVE id, so results become comparable across the ecosystem.

AVE is an open standard. The schema and records are published under Apache 2.0. AVE is moving toward neutral governance as an OWASP project — it is meant to outlive any single implementation, including Bawbel's.

Maps to OWASP MCP Top 10 · OWASP Agentic AI Top 10 · MITRE ATLAS · OWASP AIVSS v0.8. The open record set can be consumed by any tool.

Citing AVE. Reference a record by its id, which is permanent and never reused: AVE-YYYY-NNNNN: Title. Agentic Vulnerability Enumeration (AVE), Bawbel/AVE Project. https://ave.bawbel.io/AVE-YYYY-NNNNN
